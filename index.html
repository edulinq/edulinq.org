Our Mission

In short, our mission is to make educational tools for everyone. EduLinq is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to creating free and open source software for education. We believe that everyone deserves a good eduation regardless of wealth or status, and we want to help make that possible. We believe that educational software should be: 1) free 2) open source 3) high quality Free means that software is available to anyone who can download it. (Access to computers and open internet is a concern that needs to be addressed, but outside the scope of our mission.) Open source software means that we are dedicated to transparency and always improving. Anyone can see what our tools are doing, no tricks or "gotchas". Open source allows our software to always be imrpoving and evolving. It's impossible to forsee all the needs of people ay one time, but because our software is availble for all to improve, we can tackle changes as they come. High quality software means we build our software robustly and with intention. We take care to always follow strong software engineering practices, the ACM Code of Ethics, and our own moral compass. We strive build software that is secure, private, maintainable, and robust.
